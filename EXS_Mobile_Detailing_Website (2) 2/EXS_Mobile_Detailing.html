What We Do

Our Services

Our detailing services include interior cleaning, deep cleaning, car washing + waxing, paint correction, ceramic coatings, engine detailing, headlight restoration and much more. When selecting any of our detailing services you will receive friendly, professional and quality service for your car.

From prestige exotics to daily drivers and 4WDs — we tailor our protection solutions to every vehicle type and budget.

Interior Detailing

Our interior detailing service includes vacuuming, carpet and seat shampooing, stain extraction, deep cleaning of all surfaces, and conditioning — leaving your cabin fresh and revitalized.

Exterior Detailing

Our exterior detailing service goes beyond a basic wash. We safely hand wash your vehicle, remove embedded contamination using iron remover and clay bar treatment, thoroughly detail the tires, rims, and wheel wells, and apply a premium paint sealant.

Ceramic Coating

A durable, high-gloss hydrophobic coating that bonds to your paint's clear coat, offering long-term protection against UV rays, harsh weather, and contaminants — while making future washes faster and easier.

Paint Correction

A multi-stage machine polishing process that removes swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation — restoring clarity, depth, and a like-new appearance to your vehicle's paintwork.

How It Works

Simple. Seamless. Professional.

01

Quote & Booking

Give us a call or use the form online. We'll ask a few questions about the car and the type of service needed, then book a time that suits you.

02

On-Site Arrival

We arrive on time with all the gear ready. No need to supply power or water. We check the car, explain what's involved, and then we're onto it.
